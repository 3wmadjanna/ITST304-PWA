“BettaCare: A Mobile-Based Guide to Optimal Aquarium Maintenance Game.”

The study entitled “BettaCare: A Mobile-Based Guide to Optimal Aquarium Maintenance Game.” is a mobile-based game that aims to provide a comprehensive guide to optimal aquarium maintenance of Betta fish owners. The game will offer various features such as feeding the fish and tank customization that will help users learn about the proper care and maintenance of the Betta fish aquariums.
